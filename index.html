The Ceremony

1pm - 2pm


Located in the heat of the city of Belgrade, Saint Sava Cathederal, will be the venue for the ceremony and shall begin at 1pm local time lasting for an estimate of one hour.

The service will take place underneath the cathedral, not to be mistaken by the neighbouring church.

We ask that you please dress appropriately as it is a religious site.

The Reception

3pm - Late


The address of the reception venue will be disclosed to all confirmed guests after they have submitted their RSVP.

The venue will be open to accept guests from 3pm and onwards. Drinks and canapes will be served until the arrival of the Bride and Groom.

The Bride and Groom will arrive at 5pm after taking photos and proceeding other family traditions.

The Menu will consist of various Serbian delicacies. If you have any dietary requirements, please specify them when submitting your RSVP.
